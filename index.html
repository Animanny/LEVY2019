The LEVY Leadership Conference will give youth the opportunity to network with high profile speakers and learn presentation skills through case studies and Dragon’s Den, thus building their confidence level and having fun at the same time. The conference is designed for delegates to come together and make lasting friendships.

Network

Meet with and learn from some high ranking professionals in various feilds of work while also creating long lasting friendships with other highschool students.

Case Studies

Work in teams to discuss and find solutions to different case studies about various issues and topics.

Games

Having fun is just as important as working! Games that not only test your problem solving skills but, also learn to work in teams to overcome certain obstacles

Auction

Learn and participate in a virtual stock market where currency is given to you based on performance during the conference. Trade up to get more value and earn real big ticket items!

Case Studies

Learn to tackle real-life problems during case competitions, and present your ideas to win auction money and prizes

Speaker Sessions

Discover tips and tricks from industry professionals on how to tackle personal and professional challenges
